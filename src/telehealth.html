The In-Person Model Breaks Down in Small Jails

Here's how it happens

On-Call Burnout

A single nurse can be on-call 24/7, leading to turnover and inadequate coverage during emergencies.

Staffing Gaps Gap

Hiring additional staff, getting them security trained, and building clinical skills takes time—during which gaps in care put everyone at risk.

Inexperience in Corrections

Nurses with general medical experience often lack the expertise to handle correctional challenges and inmate manipulation.

Cost without Coverage

You end up paying for overhead (benefits, training, management) without getting true 24/7 coverage.
