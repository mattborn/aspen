Frequently Asked Questions

Do we need special technology to use Aspen?

No special technology is required. Aspen works with standard internet connections and basic video calling equipment that most facilities already have or can easily obtain.

What happens when an inmate complains about a medical issue?

Your staff contacts Aspen immediately. Our medical team triages the complaint and determines the appropriate level of care, taking the decision-making burden off your officers.

What if our internet is unreliable?

Aspen is designed to work with typical jail internet connections. We provide backup protocols and can work with your IT team to optimize connectivity for telehealth services.

What kinds of medical services do you provide?

Aspen provides comprehensive medical care including daily nursing triage, weekly provider visits, behavioral health sessions, 24/7 on-call access, suicide evaluations, and all administrative coordination.

Can we keep our local nurse/provider?

Aspen is designed as a complete replacement for on-site medical staff. Our telehealth model provides more reliable coverage than traditional on-site arrangements.

Do we still pay for medications?

Yes, facilities are responsible for medication costs. However, Aspen’s corrections-specialized approach typically reduces medication expenses by 20-50% through appropriate prescribing practices.

Do you offer part-time, in-person nursing or providers?

No. Aspen’s strength is our fully telehealth model that provides 24/7 coverage without the vulnerabilities of part-time or on-site arrangements.

My jail is bigger than 100 beds, do you have services for larger jails?

Aspen is specifically designed for jails under 80 beds. For larger facilities, we recommend exploring vendors that specialize in larger jail operations.

What if I have more than 100 beds, but fewer than 80 inmates on average?

Aspen evaluates each facility based on average daily population, not bed count. If your ADP consistently stays under 80, we can discuss whether Aspen is a good fit.

What does this cost?

Pricing varies based on facility size and specific needs. Contact us for a customized quote that compares costs to your current medical arrangements.

What does implementation look like?

Implementation is fast. Medical support begins on day one, with full telehealth sessions starting within the first week. Our team handles all setup and staff training.

Who does MedPass?

Your facility staff continues to handle medication distribution. Aspen manages prescribing, medication review, and all clinical decision-making around medications.

Do we need to take inmates out for labs?

Some lab work may still require transport, but Aspen significantly reduces unnecessary medical transports by handling many issues through telehealth consultations.

Do you see federal holds too?

Yes, Aspen provides medical care for all inmates in your facility regardless of their hold status or jurisdiction.
