About Aspen

We are a team of corrections-specialized clinicians and operators dedicated to solving the unique healthcare challenges of small jails.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide reliable, high-quality, and affordable medical care to inmates in small correctional facilities. We believe that every facility, regardless of size, deserves a healthcare partner they can trust. By leveraging technology and specialized expertise, we aim to reduce liability for counties, improve inmate health outcomes, and allow jail staff to focus on their core security duties.
